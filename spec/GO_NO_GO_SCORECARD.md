## Go / No-Go Scorecard (Intelligence Exchange)

Score each item 0-2. Proceed only if total >= 12/16 and no critical 0 in compliance or margins.

1. Buyer Demand Signal
- 0: low interest, no pilot intent
- 1: moderate interest, weak pilot commitment
- 2: strong pilot commitments

2. Seller Quality Signal
- 0: inconsistent/unreliable supply
- 1: limited but usable supply
- 2: stable high-quality supply

3. Compliance Confidence
- 0: unresolved major policy risks
- 1: partial controls, material ambiguity
- 2: clear operating controls and review confidence

4. Unit Economics
- 0: unattractive margins after risk/ops
- 1: marginal economics
- 2: healthy margins with realistic assumptions

5. Reliability Outcomes
- 0: frequent failures/no stable fallback
- 1: acceptable reliability for non-critical workloads
- 2: strong reliability with fallback evidence

6. GTM Efficiency
- 0: expensive acquisition/no repeatable channel
- 1: one uncertain channel
- 2: repeatable early channel with acceptable CAC

7. Dispute/Fraud Burden
- 0: high unresolved operational burden
- 1: manageable but heavy ops
- 2: controlled burden with clear processes

8. Team Execution Readiness
- 0: key capability gaps
- 1: partial readiness
- 2: ready to execute MVP roadmap
