## UX Principles (Intelligence Exchange)

1. Make economic state obvious: spend, earnings, acceptance rate, and risk flags.
2. Keep worker autonomy controllable with explicit limits and kill switch.
3. Make trust signals visible before action (worker reputation, buyer quality, task risk).
4. Minimize setup friction for first successful job on both demand and supply sides.
5. Keep failed jobs recoverable with clear requeue/retry/dispute paths.
6. Separate high-risk actions with confirmation and audit trail.

## UX Constraints
1. New workers start in constrained trust tier by default.
2. Budget, runtime window, and task-class controls are mandatory configuration.
3. Mobile UX is review/monitor first; operations-first on desktop web.
4. Dispute and settlement states must be visible without requiring log digging.
