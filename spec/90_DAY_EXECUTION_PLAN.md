## 90-Day Execution Plan (Intelligence Exchange)

## Days 0-30: Discovery + Design Partners
1. Run 15 structured interviews.
2. Secure 3-5 buyer design partners and 2-3 curated sellers.
3. Define pilot scope and operating constraints.
4. Finalize compliance baseline and risk controls.

Deliverables:
- interview synthesis
- pilot partner list
- pilot operating spec

## Days 31-60: Concierge Pilot
1. Run manual or semi-manual routing for selected workloads.
2. Capture usage, failures, dispute events, and economics.
3. Validate payment/funding UX with Stripe-first flow.
4. Apply weekly review against go/no-go scorecard.

Deliverables:
- pilot telemetry report
- interim economics report
- reliability and risk incident log

## Days 61-90: Go/No-Go + MVP Scope Freeze
1. Score pilot results against thresholds.
2. Decide continue/pivot/stop.
3. If continue: freeze MVP backlog and team plan.
4. Prepare implementation handoff package.

Deliverables:
- decision memo
- MVP scope document
- implementation roadmap

## Resource Assumptions
- 1 product lead
- 1 infra/technical lead
- 1 ops/risk lead (part-time acceptable in pilot)
