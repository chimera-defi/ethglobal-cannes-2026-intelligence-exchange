# Intelligence Exchange Decisions

## 2026-03-05 -- No credit resale
**Decision:** Do not model transferable provider credits/subscription resale.
**Rationale:** Provider terms commonly restrict transfer and account sharing.

## 2026-03-05 -- Exchange focus
**Decision:** Focus on compliant execution-capacity exchange + settlement.
**Rationale:** This is the viable path with defensible workflow value.

## 2026-03-05 -- Payments sequence
**Decision:** Stripe-first, then optional agentic and crypto rails.
**Rationale:** Maximizes launch simplicity while preserving expansion paths.

## 2026-03-05 -- Validation-first
**Decision:** Require discovery and concierge pilot before heavy build.
**Rationale:** Reduces risk on compliance, liquidity, and economics.

## 2026-03-07 -- Agent-first protocol compatibility
**Decision:** Evolve from broker-only architecture to protocol-compatible broker with adapter layer.
**Rationale:** Supports user-owned black-box agents while preserving trust, settlement, and routing control.

## 2026-03-07 -- Task market modes
**Decision:** Add explicit market modes (`claim`, `bounty`, `benchmark`, `auction`) in v2.
**Rationale:** Better fit for heterogeneous agent workloads and liquidity dynamics.
