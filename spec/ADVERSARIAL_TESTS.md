## Adversarial Tests (Intelligence Exchange)

### 1) Commodity Router Attack
**Attack:** Existing routers already solve 80% of the problem.

**Mitigation:** Win on compliance + settlement + SLA trust, not only routing.

**Kill Criterion:** Buyers prefer existing routers even with measurable savings/SLA evidence.

### 2) Non-Compliant Supply Attack
**Attack:** Sellers violate provider terms, creating legal/reputational risk.

**Mitigation:** Strict onboarding controls and policy guardrails; remove risky supply.

**Kill Criterion:** Policy violations are frequent enough to make marketplace unsafe.

### 3) Cold Start Attack
**Attack:** No liquidity on one side means no value on the other.

**Mitigation:** Start curated (design partners + selected sellers), then open up.

**Kill Criterion:** Cannot sustain healthy buyer fulfillment at pilot scale.

### 4) Margin Compression Attack
**Attack:** Price competition drives take-rate to unviable levels.

**Mitigation:** Add SLA/compliance/audit premium layers.

**Kill Criterion:** Net margins remain unattractive after ops/risk/disputes.

### 5) Payment-Risk Attack
**Attack:** Chargebacks/fraud/disputes eat economics.

**Mitigation:** Guardrails, velocity limits, staged trust tiers.

**Kill Criterion:** Fraud-adjusted gross margin falls below viability threshold.
